Frequently Asked Questions

Booking Process

To book a shipment, click "Quick Book Shipment" on your dashboard. Enter your pickup and drop locations, package details, and desired delivery date.

Payment Methods

We accept UPI, Credit/Debit Cards, and Bank Transfers. You can view and pay your pending dues in the "My Payments" section.

Shipment Tracking

Track your shipment in real-time from the "My Bookings" page. Status updates are reflected immediately once drivers update them.

Delivery Timelines

Local deliveries typically take 1-2 business days. Interstate deliveries range from 3-7 days depending on the distance. Choose Express for faster service.

Cancellation Policy

Bookings can be cancelled free of charge if the status is "Pending". Once "Approved" or "In Transit", a cancellation fee may apply.

Customer Support

Need help? Contact our support team at support@tms.local or call our toll-free number 1800-TMS-HELP.
